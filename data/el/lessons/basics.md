# The Alphabet
The Greek alphabet consists of 24 distinct graphemes. It is the foundation of Western writing. Mastery of these symbols is the first step to literacy.

## Orthographic Notes

- **Case Sensitivity**: Proper nouns and sentence starters are capitalized.
- **Question Mark**: Greek uses a semicolon (;) as a question mark.
- **Sigma**: Use **σ** normally, but **ς** at the end of a word.
