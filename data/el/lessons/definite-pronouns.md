# Definite Pronouns
Definite pronouns in Greek are used to express identity ("the same") or exclusivity ("alone / by oneself").

::: content-grid mt-8

::: content-card
ίδιος, ίδια, ίδιο
### The Same

Always used with a definite article.

Πήγα στον **ίδιο** γιατρό. I went to the **same** doctor.Έχουμε τα **ίδια** ρούχα. We have the **same** clothes.
::: end

::: content-card
μόνος, μόνη, μόνο
### Alone / By oneself

Often accompanied by a weak possessive pronoun (μου, σου...) to mean "by myself".

Το τελείωσα **μόνος μου**. I finished it **by myself**.
::: end

::: end

::: info-box mt-12 mb-12

### Vocabulary Note

Be careful not to confuse the adjective **μόνος** (alone) with the adverb **μόνο** (only). Example: Ήμουν **μόνος**. (I was **alone**) vs Έφαγα **μόνο** ένα. (I **only** ate one).

::: end
