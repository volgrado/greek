# Unit 8-10: Plurals & Verb Groups

This exercise is divided into four fluency-building activities to move you from theory to automaticity.

## 1. The Constrained Picture Exercise

*(A stylish woman trying on different coats in a high-end boutique in Athens)*
![Athens Boutique Woman](/assets/images/athens_boutique_coats.png)

**Power Lexis:**

| Greek | Pronunciation | English |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Το παλτό** -> **Τα παλτά** | *(to pal-tó)* -> *(ta pal-tá)* | The coat |
| **Μικρός, -ή, -ό** -> **Μικρά** | *(mi-krós)* -> *(mi-krá)* | Small |
| **Φοράω** | *(fo-ráo)* | I wear |
| **Ακριβός, -ή, -ό** | *(a-kri-vós)* | Expensive |

**Grammar Tags (Goals):**
1. Use **Plural Nouns and Adjectives**.
2. Conjgate a **Group B verb** (e.g. φοράω).

> **Pro Tip:** Neuter nouns ending in -ο or stressed -ό in the singular change to -α or stressed -ά in the plural. Adjectives follow the exact same pattern!

«Η γυναίκα φοράει τα μικρά παλτά. Είναι πολύ ακριβά!»
(The woman is wearing the small coats. They are very expensive!)
</details>

**Why it works:**

* **τα μικρά παλτά / πολύ ακριβά**: "Το παλτό" becomes "Τα παλτά" in the plural. Accordingly, the adjectives "μικρό" and "ακριβό" change their endings to match the neuter plural form (μικρά, ακριβά).
* **φοράει**: "Φοράω" belongs to Verb Group B1 (verbs ending in an unstressed -άω in the "I" form). The "she" form takes the -άει ending.

## 3. The Mutation Drill (Speed-Shift)

Read the "Base" sentence aloud. Then, look at the "Trigger" and instantly change the sentence mentally before checking the Target. This builds agility without overthinking.

| Base Sentence | Trigger | Target Sentence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Η γυναίκα φοράει το παλτό.** <br> *(The woman wears the coat.)* | Change object to **Plural** | **Η γυναίκα φοράει τα παλτά.** |
| **Το μικρό παιδί παίζει.** <br> *(The small child plays.)* | Change subject to **Plural** | **Τα μικρά παιδιά παίζουν.** |
| **Αγοράζω το ακριβό ρούχο.** <br> *(I buy the expensive piece of clothing.)* | Change object to **Plural** | **Αγοράζω τα ακριβά ρούχα.** |
| **Ο πωλητής μιλάει πολύ.** <br> *(The salesperson talks a lot.)* | Change subject to **Plural** | **Οι πωλητές μιλούν πολύ.** |

---

## 4. The Grammar-Saturated Story

Read this short story three times: first for accuracy, second for comprehension, and third *as fast as possible*. This forces your brain to "soak" in the patterns of Plural Nouns and Group B Verbs.

> «Τα μαγαζιά είναι ακριβά. Οι γυναίκες αγαπούν τα ωραία ρούχα. Δοκιμάζουν τα μικρά φορέματα και τα μεγάλα παλτά. Οι πωλητές μιλούν πολύ. Τα ρούχα κοστίζουν πολλά ευρώ. Όλοι γελάνε.»
