# Essential Phrases
Mastering these core phrases will help you navigate your first interactions in Greek. Practice saying them out loud to improve your pronunciation!

::: info-box
### Survival Scenario: Ordering a Coffee (Παραγγέλνοντας καφέ)
In Greece, coffee is a social ritual. Use these phrases to get your fix!

- **Έναν καφέ, παρακαλώ.** (One coffee, please.)
- **Ένα φρέντο εσπρέσο μέτριο.** (One Freddo Espresso, medium sweetness.)
- **Πόσο κάνει;** (How much is it?)
- **Ορίστε.** (Here you go.)

::: end

### Greetings

- Γειά σου! Hello / Hi (informal)
- Γειά σας! Hello (formal/plural)
- Καλημέρα! Good morning!
- Καλησπέρα! Good evening!

::: end

::: content-card

### Basic Interaction

- Τι κάνεις; How are you?
- Καλά είμαι. I am well.
- Πώς σε λένε; What is your name?
- Με λένε... My name is...

::: end

::: content-card

### Courtesy

- Ευχαριστώ. Thank you.
- Παρακαλώ. Please / You're welcome.
- Συγγνώμη. I'm sorry / Excuse me.
- Ναι / Όχι Yes / No

::: end

::: end

::: info-box mt-12 mb-12

### Did you know?

The word **Παρακαλώ** is extremely versatile. It means "please", but also "you're welcome", and sometimes "I'm sorry?" or "Yes, can I help you?".

::: end
