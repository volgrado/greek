### 🔍 Reading Analysis

Did you spot the patterns from Unit 1?

*   **Vowel Combinations (The "EE" Sound):** The words φτάν**ει**, κοιτάζ**ει**, **εί**ναι, and ξενοδοχ**εί**ο all use **ει** to make the "EE" sound.
*   **Vowel Combinations (The "EH" Sound):** The words κ**αι** and **αί**νιγμα use **αι** to make the "EH" sound (like "egg").
*   **Vowel Combinations (The "OO" Sound):** The words υπάρχ**ου**ν, **ου**ρανό, and σ**ου** use **ου** para formar el sonido "U" (como en "luna").
*   **Consonant Digraphs:** Did you notice the **τσ** in *τσ*ιμεντένια, the **τζ** in *τζ*άκετ, the **μπ** in *μπ*λοκ, the **γκ** in *γκ*ρίζα, and the **ντ** in *εντ*άξει?

**Rule Reminder:** Whenever you see these combinations, they merge to form a single, completely new sound. Do not pronounce their letters separately!
