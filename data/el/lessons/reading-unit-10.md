### 🔍 Reading Analysis

**Adverbs of Place** are essential for describing locations and directions. In Greek, many of these adverbs are used alone or with prepositions.

| Greek Adverb | English Meaning | Opposite |
|---|---|---|
| **Εδώ** | Here | **Εκεί** (There) |
| **Πάνω** | Up / On | **Κάτω** (Down / Under) |
| **Μέσα** | Inside | **Έξω** (Outside) |
| **Μπροστά** | In front | **Πίσω** (Behind) |
| **Κοντά** | Near | **Μακριά** (Far) |

**Usage Note:**
- When using adverbs like **πάνω**, **κάτω**, **μπροστά**, **πίσω**, **κοντά**, **μακριά** with a noun, we usually add the preposition **από** or **σε**.
    - **Πάνω στο** τραπέζι (On the table)
    - **Κοντά στο** σπίτι (Near the house)
    - **Μακριά από** την πόλη (Far from the city)
