# Unit 12: Building the Simple Stem (Σ, Ξ, Ψ)

To speak in the Future or the Subjunctive, you need the **Simple Stem** (the "Photo" stem). Finding this stem is like a puzzle: you look at the ending of the verb and swap it for a new one. 

There are three main "Cheat Codes" to master.

---

## 1. The "S" Group (Σ) ➔ Most common
If the verb ends in **-νω, -ζω, -ω,** or **-θω**, the stem usually changes to a **-σ-**.

- **διαβάζω** (read) ➔ **διαβάσ-**
- **κλείνω** (close) ➔ **κλείσ-**
- **πληρώνω** (pay) ➔ **πληρώσ-**
- **νιώθω** (feel) ➔ **νιώσ-**

---

## 2. The "X" Group (Ξ) ➔ Throat sounds
If the verb ends in a "throat" sound like **-κω, -γω, -χω**, or a specific **-ζω**, it explodes into a **-ξ-** (Xi).

- **ανοίγω** (open) ➔ **ανοίξ-**
- **τρέχω** (run) ➔ **τρέξ-**
- **διδάσκω** (teach) ➔ **διδάξ-**
- **παίζω** (play) ➔ **παίξ-** (The main exception!)

---

## 3. The "PS" Group (Ψ) ➔ Lip sounds
If the verb ends in a "lip" sound like **-πω, -βω, -φω**, it explodes into a **-ψ-** (Psi).

- **γράφω** (write) ➔ **γράψ-**
- **λείπω** (be absent) ➔ **λείψ-**
- **κρύβω** (hide) ➔ **κρύψ-**

---

## 4. Why does this matter?
Now that you have the stem (e.g., **γράψ-**), you can use it for everything you learned in Unit 10 and 11:

- **Future**: Θα **γράψ**ω. (I will write).
- **Subjunctive**: Θέλω να **γράψ**ω. (I want to write).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Irregulars**  
> Some verbs are rebels and don't follow these codes. You just have to memorize their simple stems:
> - **βλέπω** (see) ➔ **ιδ-** (θα δω)
> - **λέω** (say) ➔ **π-** (θα πω)
> - **πάω** (go) ➔ **πα-** (θα πάω)
> - **έρχομαι** (come) ➔ **ερθ-** (θα έρθω)
