# Social Expressions
This lesson covers common expressions used in social settings, including answering the phone and social events.

::: content-grid mt-8

### On the Phone

- Εμπρός; (Hello? - answering)
- Ναι; (Yes? / Hello?)
- Ποιος είναι; (Who is it?)
- Ένα λεπτό. (One minute.)

### Congratulations & Wishes

- Χρόνια Πολλά! (Happy Birthday/Anniversary!)
- Συγχαρητήρια! (Congratulations!)
- Καλή επιτυχία! (Good luck!)
- Περαστικά! (Get well soon!)

### Drinks & Food

- Στην υγειά μας! (Cheers!)
- Γειά μας! (Cheers!)
- Καλή όρεξη! (Bon Appétit!)
::: end

::: info-box mt-12 mb-12

### Cultural Note

**Χρόνια Πολλά** is the most common wish in Greece. It is used for birthdays, name days, weddings, Christmas, Easter, and almost every national holiday!

::: end
