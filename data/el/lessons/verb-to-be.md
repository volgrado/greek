# The Verb 'To Be' (Είμαι)
The verb 'to be' is arguably the most important verb in any language. In Greek, it is irregular but follows a consistent pattern of endings.

## 1. Present Indicative

| Person | Greek | English |
|---|---|---|
| I am | Εγώ είμαι | I am |
| You are | Εσύ είσαι | You are |
| He/She/It is | Αυτός/ή/ό είναι | He/She/It is |
| We are | Εμείς είμαστε | We are |
| You (pl/formal) | Εσείς είστε | You are |
| They are | Αυτοί/ές/ά είναι | They are |
