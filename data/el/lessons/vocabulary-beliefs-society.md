# B2 Vocabulary: Beliefs, Relations & Society

Vocabulary designed to pair with Relative/Reflexive pronouns and advanced interpersonal discussions.

## 1. Reflexives & Relations
- **ο εαυτός μου** *(o e-af-tós moo)* = myself (used constantly in B2!)
- **ο ένας τον άλλον** *(o é-nas ton ál-lon)* = each other / one another
- **η σχέση** *(ee shé-see)* = the relationship
- **το πρόβλημα** *(to pró-vlee-ma)* = the problem
- **ο κόσμος** *(o kós-mos)* = the world / the "people"

## 2. Expressing Beliefs & Abstract Verbs
- **πιστεύω** *(pis-tév-o)* = to believe
- **νομίζω** *(no-mí-zo)* = to think (as an opinion: *Νομίζω ότι...*)
- **συμφωνώ** *(sim-fo-nó)* = to agree
- **διαφωνώ** *(dya-fo-nó)* = to disagree
- **ελπίζω** *(el-pí-zo)* = to hope
- **αλλάζω** *(a-lá-zo)* = to change

## 3. Abstract Nouns for Conversation
- **η ζωή** *(ee zo-í)* = the life
- **η αλήθεια** *(ee a-lí-thya)* = the truth
- **το ψέμα** *(to psé-ma)* = the lie
- **η ιδέα** *(ee ee-dé-a)* = the idea (Plural: *οι ιδέες*)
- **η δουλειά** *(ee doo-lyá)* = the work / job (Plural: *οι δουλειές*)
