# B1 Vocabulary: Deponent Verbs & States

Master the most common **-ομαι** verbs that describe thoughts, feelings, and body states.

## 1. Thoughts & Memories
- **σκέφτομαι** *(ské-fto-me)* = to think / to consider
- **θυμάμαι** *(thee-má-me)* = to remember
- **ξεχνάω** *(xe-hná-o)* = to forget (Active counterpart!)
- **αισθάνομαι** *(es-thá-no-me)* = to feel

## 2. Daily Routines (Reflexive actions)
- **κοιμάμαι** *(kee-má-me)* = to sleep
- **σηκώνομαι** *(see-kó-no-me)* = to get up / stand up
- **κάθομαι** *(ká-tho-me)* = to sit
- **πλένομαι** *(plé-no-me)* = to wash oneself
- **λούζομαι** *(loo-zo-me)* = to wash one's hair

## 3. Interaction
- **έρχομαι** *(ér-ho-me)* = to come (Aorist: *ήρθα*)
- **γίνομαι** *(yí-no-me)* = to become / to happen
- **χρειάζομαι** *(hree-á-zo-me)* = to need
- **δανείζομαι** *(da-ní-zo-me)* = to borrow
