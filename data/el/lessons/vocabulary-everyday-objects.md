# A1 Vocabulary: Everyday Objects & Places

Words for the things and places around you.

![Greek Coffee Table](/assets/images/vocab_objects.png)

## 1. Top Objects
- **το βιβλίο** *(to vee-vlí-o)* = book
- **η τσάντα** *(ee tsán-da)* = bag
- **η πόρτα** *(ee pór-ta)* = door
- **το αυτοκίνητο** *(to af-to-kí-nee-to)* = car

## 2. Food & Drink
- **το νερό** *(to ne-ró)* = water
- **ο καφές** *(o ka-fés)* = coffee
- **η ζάχαρη** *(ee zá-ha-ree)* = sugar

## 3. Places & Environment
- **το σπίτι** *(to spí-tee)* = house / home
- **η οδός** *(ee o-dós)* = street
- **το σινεμά** *(to see-ne-má)* = cinema
- **ο ουρανός** *(o oo-ra-nós)* = sky
- **η θάλασσα** *(ee thá-las-sa)* = sea
