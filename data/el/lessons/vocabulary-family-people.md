# A1 Vocabulary: Family & People

Learn the essential words to talk about people and family members in Greek.

![Greek Family Dinner](/assets/images/vocab_family.png)

## 1. Immediate Family (Οικογένεια)
- **ο πατέρας** *(o pa-té-ras)* = father
- **η μητέρα** *(ee mee-té-ra)* = mother
- **ο μπαμπάς** *(o ba-bás)* = dad
- **το παιδί** *(to pe-dí)* = child

## 2. People (Άνθρωποι)
- **ο άντρας** *(o án-dras)* = man / husband
- **η γυναίκα** *(ee yee-né-ka)* = woman / wife
- **ο φίλος** *(o fí-los)* = friend (male)
- **η φίλη** *(ee fí-lee)* = friend (female)

## 3. Professionals
- **ο δάσκαλος / η δασκάλα** *(o dás-ka-los / ee das-ká-la)* = teacher
- **ο/η γιατρός** *(o/ee ya-trós)* = doctor
