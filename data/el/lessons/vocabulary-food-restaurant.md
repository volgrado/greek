# A2 Vocabulary: Food & the Restaurant

Vocabulary meant to be combined with the *Simple Past* (I ate) and *Subjunctive* (I want to eat) alongside plural objects.

## 1. Restaurant Basics
- **το εστιατόριο** *(to e-stya-tó-ryo)* = the restaurant
- **η ταβέρνα** *(ee ta-vér-na)* = the tavern
- **το μενού / ο κατάλογος** *(to me-noo / o ka-tá-lo-gos)* = the menu / catalog
- **το πιάτο** *(to pyá-to)* = the plate (Plural: τα πιάτα)
- **το ποτήρι** *(to po-tí-ree)* = the glass (Plural: τα ποτήρια)

## 2. Eating and Drinking Verbs (Aorist Stem changes)
- **τρώω** *(tró-o)* = to eat (Aorist: *έφαγα* / Subjunctive: *να φάω*)
- **πίνω** *(pí-no)* = to drink (Aorist: *ήπια* / Subjunctive: *να πιω*)
- **παραγγέλνω** *(pa-ran-gél-no)* = to order 

## 3. Essential Foods
- **το ψωμί** *(to pso-mí)* = the bread
- **το κρέας** *(to kré-as)* = the meat
- **το ψάρι** *(to psá-ree)* = the fish
- **το τυρί** *(to tee-rí)* = the cheese
- **τα φρούτα** *(ta froo-ta)* = the fruits (usually used in plural)
- **τα λαχανικά** *(ta la-ha-nee-ká)* = the vegetables

## 4. Useful Phrases
- **Τι θα πάρετε;** *(ti tha pá-re-te?)* = What will you have/take?
- **Μπορώ να έχω...;** *(bo-ró na é-ho...)* = Can I have...?
