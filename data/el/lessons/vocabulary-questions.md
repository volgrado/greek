# A1 Vocabulary: Question Words & Pronouns

Mastering how to ask for things.

![Confusing Athens Crossroads](/assets/images/vocab_questions.png)

## 1. The "W" Words 
- **Τι;** *(ti)* = What?
- **Πού;** *(poo)* = Where?
- **Πότε;** *(pó-te)* = When?
- **Πώς;** *(pos)* = How?
- **Γιατί;** *(ya-tí)* = Why?

## 2. Chameleon Question Words
- **Ποιος / Ποια / Ποιο;** *(pyos / pya / pyo)* = Who? / Which?
- **Πόσο;** *(pó-so)* = How much?
- **Πόσα;** *(pó-sa)* = How many?

## 3. Pronouns & Particles
- **εγώ** *(e-gó)* = I
- **εσύ** *(e-sí)* = you (singular)
- **αυτός / αυτή / αυτό** *(af-tós / af-tí / af-tó)* = he / she / it
- **εμείς** *(e-mís)* = we
- **εσείς** *(e-sís)* = you (plural)
- **δεν** *(den)* = not
