# A2 Vocabulary: Shopping & Clothes

Perfect for practicing plurals, adjective agreement, and object pronouns ("I want *those*", "Give *me it*").

## 1. Shopping Locations
- **το μαγαζί / το κατάστημα** *(to ma-ga-zí / to ka-tá-stee-ma)* = the shop / store
- **η αγορά** *(ee a-go-rá)* = the market
- **το σούπερ μάρκετ** *(to soo-per már-ket)* = the supermarket

## 2. Verbs for Shopping
- **αγοράζω** *(a-go-rá-zo)* = to buy (Simple Stem: αγοράσ-)
- **πουλάω** *(poo-lá-o)* = to sell (Type B1 verb)
- **κοστίζει** *(ko-stí-zee)* = it costs
- **ψάχνω** *(psáh-no)* = to look for / to search

## 3. Clothes (Τα ρούχα)
*Notice how many clothes are naturally plural, just like in English!*

- **τα ρούχα** *(ta roo-ha)* = the clothes
- **το παντελόνι** *(to pan-de-ló-nee)* = the trousers / pants (Plural: τα παντελόνια)
- **το παπούτσι** *(to pa-poo-tsee)* = the shoe (Plural: τα παπούτσια)
- **η μπλούζα** *(ee bloo-za)* = the blouse / t-shirt
- **το φόρεμα** *(to fó-re-ma)* = the dress
- **το μπουφάν** *(to boo-fán)* = the jacket

## 4. Money & Descriptors
- **τα λεφτά / τα χρήματα** *(ta lef-tá / ta hrí-ma-ta)* = the money
- **ακριβός, -ή, -ό** *(ak-ree-vós)* = expensive
- **φθηνός, -ή, -ό** *(fthee-nós)* = cheap
- **μεγάλος, -η, -ο** *(me-gá-los)* = big
- **μικρός, -ή, -ό** *(mik-rós)* = small
