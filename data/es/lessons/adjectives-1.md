# Adjectives I
In Spanish, adjectives must agree in both gender and number with the nouns they describe.

## Agreement in Gender

Adjectives ending in **-o** change to **-a** for feminine nouns.

- el gato negro (the black cat)
- la gata negra (the black cat (female))

## Agreement in Number

Adjectives follow the same rules as nouns for making plural forms.

- los gatos negros (the black cats)
- las gatas negras (the black cats (female))

::: info-box

### Exception!

Adjectives that end in **-e** or a consonant don't change for gender, only for number.

el libro grande / la casa grande

::: end
