# Adjectives II
In Spanish, the position of an adjective is just as important as its form.

## Standard Placement

Most adjectives follow the noun they modify, which is the opposite of English.

- el auto rojo (the red car)
- English: red car → Spanish: car red

## Exceptions: Determinative Adjectives

Adjectives that indicate quantity or order often precede the noun.

- muchos libros (many books)
- la primera vez (the first time)

### Aesthetic Placement

In poetry or literature, adjectives may precede the noun to add emphasis or a descriptive quality, but in daily speech, they almost always follow the noun.
