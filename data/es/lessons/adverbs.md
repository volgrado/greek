# Adverbs
Adverbs describe how, when, or where an action occurs. Many Spanish adverbs are formed from adjectives.

## The -mente Ending

To form an adverb from an adjective, use the feminine singular form and add **-mente** (equivalent to -ly in English).

| Adjective | Adverb |
|---|---|
| Rápido (Fast) | Rápidamente |
| Lento (Slow) | Lentamente |
| Fácil (Easy) | Fácilmente |

### Placement

Adverbs that modify verbs usually come **after** the verb. Adverbs that modify adjectives or other adverbs come **before** the word they modify.
