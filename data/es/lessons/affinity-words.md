# Affirmative and Negative Words
Spanish uses pairs of words to affirm or deny the existence of people or things.

## Pairings

| Affirmative | Negative |
|---|---|
| Algo (Something) | Nada (Nothing) |
| Alguien (Someone) | Nadie (No one) |
| Siempre (Always) | Nunca (Never) |
| También (Also) | Tampoco (Neither) |

### Double Negatives

In English, double negatives are avoided. In Spanish, they are required!

No veo a nadie.

I don't see anyone (I don't see no one).
