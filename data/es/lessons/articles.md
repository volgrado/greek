# Definite and Indefinite Articles
Spanish articles must agree in gender and number with the nouns they modify.

## Definite Articles (The)

| Number | Masculine | Feminine |
|---|---|---|
| Singular | el | la |
| Plural | los | las |

## Indefinite Articles (A, An, Some)

| Number | Masculine | Feminine |
|---|---|---|
| Singular | un | una |
| Plural | unos | unas |
