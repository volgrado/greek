# Object Pronouns with Commands
The position of object pronouns changes depending on whether a command is affirmative or negative.

## Affirmative Commands

In affirmative commands, pronouns are **attached** to the end of the verb.

Dí**melo**. (Tell it to me.)

Cómpre**lo**. (Buy it.)

Note: Adding a pronoun often requires adding an accent mark to maintain the original stress.

## Negative Commands

In negative commands, pronouns come **before** the verb (and after 'no').

No **me lo** diga. (Don't tell it to me.)

No **lo** compre. (Don't buy it.)
