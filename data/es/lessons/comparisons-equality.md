# Comparisons of Equality
To compare things that are equal (as... as), Spanish uses different formulas depending on whether you are using adjectives or nouns.

## With Adjectives

Formula: **tan** + [adjective] + **como**

Ella es tan inteligente como él.

She is as intelligent as him.

## With Nouns

Formula: **tanto/a/os/as** + [noun] + **como**

Notice that 'tanto' must agree in gender and number with the noun.

- Tengo tantos libros como tú. (I have as many books as you.)
- Hay tanta gente como ayer. (There is as much people (as many people) as yesterday.)
