# Conditional Tense
The conditional tense is used to express what **would** happen. Like the future, endings are added to the infinitive.

## The Endings

Interestingly, the conditional endings are identical to the imperfect endings for -er and -ir verbs.

| yo | -ía |
|---|---|
| tú | -ías |
| él/ella | -ía |
| nosotros | -íamos |
| ellos/ellas | -ían |

## Examples

- Comería (I would eat)
- Viajaríamos (We would travel)
- Tendría (I would have - using irreg. stem)
