# Definite Article II
In Spanish, the definite article (el, la, los, las) is used in several situations where it is omitted in English.

## Special Uses

- **With abstract nouns**: El amor es importante. (Love is important.)
- **With days of the week**: El lunes voy al cine. (On Monday I'm going to the cinema.)
- **With titles (when talking about someone)**: El doctor García está aquí. (Dr. Garcia is here.)
- **With body parts and clothing** (replaces possessive adjectives): Me lavo las manos. (I wash my hands.)
