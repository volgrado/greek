# Direct Object Pronouns III
Just like with infinitives, direct object pronouns can also be attached to present particles (-ando, -iendo).

## Placement with Particles

Option 1: Before the conjugated verb (estar).

**Lo** estoy leyendo.

Option 2: Attached to the end of the participle.

Estoy leyéndo**lo**.

### Accent Alert!

Notice that when you attach a pronoun to a present participle, you must add an accent mark to the original stressed vowel of the participle to maintain the correct stress.

leyendo → leyéndolo
