# Formal Commands
Formal commands are used with people you address as 'usted' or 'ustedes' (strangers, superiors, groups).

## The Formula

Step 1: Start with the **yo** form of the present tense.

Step 2: Drop the **-o**.

Step 3: Add the 'opposite' ending.

| Verb Group | Usted | Ustedes |
|---|---|---|
| -ar verbs | -e | -en |
| -er / -ir verbs | -a | -an |

### Examples

- Hablar → yo hablo → **Hable** / **Hablen**
- Comer → yo como → **Coma** / **Coman**
- Escribir → yo escribo → **Escriba** / **Escriban**
