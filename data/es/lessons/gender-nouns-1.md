# Gender of Nouns I
In Spanish, all nouns have a grammatical gender: they are either masculine or feminine.

## Basic Rules

While there are exceptions, the most common way to identify the gender of a noun is by its ending:

::: content-grid

::: content-card

### Masculine Nouns

Nouns that end in **-o** are usually masculine.

- el chico (the boy)
- el libro (the book)

::: end

::: content-card

### Feminine Nouns

Nouns that end in **-a** are usually feminine.

- la chica (the girl)
- la pluma (the pen)

::: end

::: end

::: info-box

### Pro Tip!

Always learn the noun with its article (el or la). This is the most reliable way to remember its gender.

::: end
