# Gender of Nouns II
Beyond -o and -a, there are other common endings that can help you determine a noun's gender.

## Additional Feminine Endings

Nouns ending in these suffixes are almost always feminine:

| Ending | Example | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **-ción** | la lección | the lesson |
| **-tad** | la libertad | the liberty |
| **-dad** | la ciudad | the city |

## Common Exceptions: Masculine -ma / -pa

Nouns of Greek origin ending in **-ma** or **-pa** are usually masculine, even though they end in -a.

- el problema (the problem)
- el mapa (the map)
- el sistema (the system)
