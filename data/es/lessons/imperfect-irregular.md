# Imperfect: Irregular Verbs
Great news! There are only **three** irregular verbs in the imperfect tense.

## Ser, Ir, and Ver

| Person | Ser | Ir | Ver |
|---|---|---|---|
| yo | era | iba | veía |
| tú | eras | ibas | veías |
| él/ella | era | iba | veía |
| nosotros | éramos | íbamos | veíamos |
| ellos | eran | iban | veían |

### Note on Ver

Ver is only 'slightly' irregular because it keeps the 'e' from the infinitive (veía instead of vía).
