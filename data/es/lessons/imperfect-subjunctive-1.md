# Imperfect Subjunctive I
The imperfect subjunctive is used in the same situations as the present subjunctive (WEIRDO), but when the main verb is in the past tense or conditional.

## The Formula

To form the imperfect subjunctive, you always start with the **third-person plural (ellos/ellas)** form of the **Preterite**.

Take the 'ellos' preterite form (e.g., hablaron, comieron).Drop the **-ron**.Add the imperfect subjunctive endings.
## The Endings

| yo | -ra |
|---|---|
| tú | -ras |
| él/ella | -ra |
| nosotros | -ramos |
| ellos/ellas | -ran |

::: info-box

### Accent Rule

In the 'nosotros' form, you must add an accent mark to the vowel immediately preceding the ending: **habláramos, comiéramos**.

::: end
