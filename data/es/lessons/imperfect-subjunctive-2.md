# Imperfect Subjunctive II
Because the imperfect subjunctive is based on the 'ellos' preterite form, any verb that is irregular in the preterite will have the same irregularity here.

## Irregular Examples

| Verb | Ellos Preterite | Subj. Stem | Yo Form |
|---|---|---|---|
| Estar | estuvieron | estuvie- | estuviera |
| Hacer | hicieron | hicie- | hiciera |
| Ir/Ser | fueron | fue- | fuera |
| Tener | tuvieron | tuvie- | tuviera |

### Alternate Endings

In some regions (especially Spain) and in literature, you may see an alternative set of endings: -se, -ses, -se, -semos, -seis, -sen. Both are correct, but -ra is more common in speech.
