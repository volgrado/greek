# Irregular Present Participles
Some verbs have irregular present participles, often due to spelling changes or stem-shifts.

## The '-yendo' Group

When the stem of an -er or -ir verb ends in a vowel, use **-yendo** instead of -iendo.

- Leer → **leyendo**
- Oír → **oyendo**
- Traer → **trayendo**

## Stem-Changing Verbs

Only -ir stem-changing verbs keep their change in the participle.

- e → i: **Diciendo** (from decir)
- o → u: **Durmiendo** (from dormir)
