# Verbs with Irregular Yo
Some common Spanish verbs are regular in all forms except for the first-person singular (yo).

## The 'Go' Verbs

These verbs add a **g** in the yo form.

| Infinitive | Yo Form | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| hacer | hago | I do/make |
| poner | pongo | I put |
| salir | salgo | I leave |
| traer | traigo | I bring |

## The 'Yo-oy' Verbs

- dar → doy ((I give))
- ir → voy ((I go))
