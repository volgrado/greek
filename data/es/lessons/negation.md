# Negation
In Spanish, making a sentence negative is much simpler than in English. You just add **no** before the verb.

## Rule & Examples

::: example-box
Positive: Yo hablo español.

Negative: Yo **no** hablo español.

::: end

- No estoy cansado. (I am not tired.)
- Ellos no son de aquí. (They are not from here.)

### Answer with No

When answering a question in the negative, you often say **no** twice: once for the 'No' and once for the sentence negation.

—¿Hablas inglés? —No, no hablo inglés.
