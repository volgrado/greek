# Numbers: 11-100
As you go higher, Spanish numbers follow a pattern, especially after 20.

## The Tricky Teens

- 11 — Once
- 12 — Doce
- 13 — Trece
- 14 — Catorce
- 15 — Quince

## Tens and Patterns

| Number | Spanish |
|---|---|
| 20 | veinte |
| 30 | treinta |
| 40 | cuarenta |
| 50 | cincuenta |
| 100 | cien |

::: info-box

### Building Numbers

For numbers between tens, use 'y' (and) for 31 and above: **treinta y uno** (31). Between 21 and 29, it's one word: **veintiuno** (21).

::: end
