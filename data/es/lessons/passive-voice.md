# The Passive Voice
In the passive voice, the subject of the sentence receives the action rather than performing it (The book was written).

## Option 1: SER + Past Participle

This is used when the person performing the action (the agent) is mentioned using 'por'.

El libro **fue escrito por** Cervantes.

Note: The participle must agree in gender and number with the subject.

## Option 2: The Passive 'Se'

This is far more common in everyday Spanish when the agent is unknown or unimportant.

**Se vende** pan aquí.

Bread is sold here.
