# Pero vs Sino
Spanish has two words for 'but', and they are used in very specific contexts.

## Pero

The standard word for 'but'. It adds information that contrasts with the first part of the sentence.

Es caro pero bueno. (It's expensive but good.)

## Sino

Used after a **negative** statement to mean 'but rather' or 'on the contrary'.

No es azul **sino** verde.

It's not blue but (rather) green.
