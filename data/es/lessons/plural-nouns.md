# Plural Forms of Nouns
Making nouns plural in Spanish is a straightforward process based on whether the word ends in a vowel or a consonant.

## Rule 1: Ending in a Vowel

If a noun ends in a vowel, simply add **-s**.

- el libro → los libros (the books)
- la pluma → las plumas (the pens)

## Rule 2: Ending in a Consonant

If a noun ends in a consonant, add **-es**.

- el borrador → los borradores (the erasers)
- la ciudad → las ciudades (the cities)

### Nouns ending in -z

If a noun ends in **z**, change the **z** to **c** before adding **-es**.

el lápiz → los lápices
