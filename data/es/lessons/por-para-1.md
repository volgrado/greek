# Por vs Para I
In English, both 'por' and 'para' are often translated as 'for.' However, in Spanish, they have distinct uses that are never interchangeable.

## Primary Uses of POR

Think of **por** as expressing movement through a space, duration of time, or cause/reason.

- **Motion or general location** (through, along, by, or in the area of)
- **Duration of an action** (for, during, in)
- **Reason or motive** (because of, on account of, on behalf of)
- **Exchange or substitution** (for, in exchange for)

## Examples of Por

- Paseamos por el parque. (We walked through the park (Motion).)
- Estudié por dos horas. (I studied for two hours (Duration).)
- Gracias por el regalo. (Thank you for the gift (Exchange).)
