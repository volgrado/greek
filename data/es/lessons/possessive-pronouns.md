# Possessive Pronouns
Possessive pronouns are used to show ownership without repeating the noun (mine, yours, theirs).

## The Forms

Possessive pronouns must agree in gender and number with the noun being replaced.

| Person | Masc. Sing. | Fem. Sing. |
|---|---|---|
| Yo | mío | mía |
| Tú | tuyo | tuya |
| Él/Ella | suyo | suya |
| Nosotros | nuestro | nuestra |

## Using them with Articles

In Spanish, you usually include the definite article.

::: example-box
Este libro es el mío. (This book is mine.)

Tengo mi pluma y la tuya. (I have my pen and yours.)

::: end
