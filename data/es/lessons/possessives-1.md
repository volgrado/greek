# Possessive Adjectives I
Possessive adjectives indicate ownership or relationship. In Spanish, they must agree in number with the noun they modify.

## Short-Form Possessive Adjectives

| Person | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| My | mi | mis |
| Your (inf.) | tu | tus |
| His/Her/Your (for.) | su | sus |

::: info-box

### Agreement Rule

Possessive adjectives agree with the **possessed object**, not the possessor.

mi libro / mis libros

::: end
