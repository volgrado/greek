# Possessive Adjectives II
For 'our' and 'your' (plural), the possessive adjectives also agree in gender.

## Nosotros/as & Vosotros/as

| Adjective | Masc. Sing. | Fem. Sing. | Masc. Plur. | Fem. Plur. |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Our | nuestro | nuestra | nuestros | nuestras |
| Your (pl. inf.) | vuestro | vuestra | vuestros | vuestras |

### Their / Your (Formal Plural)

For 'their' (ellos/ellas) and 'your' (ustedes), we reuse **su** and **sus**.

su casa / sus libros
