# Preterite vs Imperfect I
Deciding between preterite and imperfect is one of the biggest challenges in Spanish. It depends on how you view the action.

## The Preterite (The 'Box')

Use the preterite for actions that are **complete** or have a clear beginning and end.

- Specific events
- List of sequential actions

## The Imperfect (The 'Line')

Use the imperfect for actions that were **ongoing**, habitual, or descriptive.

- Characteristics/Descriptions
- Time, Age, Weather
- Habitual actions (Used to)

::: example-box
Preterite: Comí a las dos. (I ate at 2:00).

Imperfect: Comía mucho de niño. (I used to eat a lot as a child).

::: end
