# Preterite: Irregular Verbs I
Some of the most common verbs in Spanish are completely irregular in the preterite. Interestingly, 'ser' and 'ir' share the exact same forms!

## Ir & Ser (To go / To be)

| yo | fui |
|---|---|
| tú | fuiste |
| él/ella | fue |
| nosotros | fuimos |
| ellos/ellas | fueron |

## Dar (To give)

| yo | di |
|---|---|
| tú | diste |
| él/ella | dio |
| nosotros | dimos |
| ellos/ellas | dieron |

## Ver (To see)

Ver follows the same pattern as 'dar', but with a 'v'.

vi, viste, vio, vimos, vieron
