# Preterite: Irregular Verbs II
Many irregular preterite verbs follow a pattern: they have a special stem and share the same set of endings (which have no accent marks!).

## Verbs: Estar, Tener, Hacer

- Estar → **estuv-**
- Tener → **tuv-**
- Hacer → **hic-** (hizo in 3rd pers. sing.)

## The Shared Endings

| yo | -e |
|---|---|
| tú | -iste |
| él/ella | -o |
| nosotros | -imos |
| ellos/ellas | -ieron |

### Example: Tener

tuve, tuviste, tuvo, tuvimos, tuvieron
