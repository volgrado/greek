# Preterite: Irregular Verbs IV
The final group of irregular stems, including those that use a 'j' in their stem and have a slightly different plural ending.

## Verbs: Querer, Venir, Decir

- Querer → **quis-** (to want)
- Venir → **vin-** (to come)
- Decir → **dij-** (to say/tell)

## The 'J' Stem Rule

For verbs with a 'j' in the stem (like decir), the third-person plural ending is **-eron** (without the 'i').

::: example-box
Decir: dije, dijiste, dijo, dijimos, **dijeron**

Traer: traje, trajiste, trajo, trajimos, **trajeron**

::: end
