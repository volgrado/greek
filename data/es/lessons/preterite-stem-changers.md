# Preterite: Stem-Changers
In the preterite, only **-ir** verbs that change their stem in the present tense also change in the past.

## The Rule

Stem-changing happens only in the **third-person** forms (él/ella/usted and ellos/ellas/ustedes).

- e → i: **Pedir** (to ask for)
- o → u: **Dormir** (to sleep)

## Conjugation of Pedir (e → i)

| yo | pedí |
|---|---|
| tú | pediste |
| él/ella | p**i**dió |
| nosotros | pedimos |
| ellos/ellas | p**i**dieron |

## Conjugation of Dormir (o → u)

| yo | dormí |
|---|---|
| tú | dormiste |
| él/ella | d**u**rmió |
| nosotros | dormimos |
| ellos/ellas | d**u**rmieron |
