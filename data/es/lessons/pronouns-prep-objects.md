# Pronouns: Object of Prepositions
When a pronoun follows a preposition (like for, with, about), Spanish uses specific forms that are mostly identical to subject pronouns.

## The Pronoun Forms

| Subject | Object of Preposition |
|---|---|
| Yo | mí |
| Tú | ti |
| Él / Ella / Ud. | él / ella / ud. |
| Nosotros | nosotros |

::: info-box

### Special Cases with 'Con'

When used with 'con' (with), 'mí' and 'ti' become:

conmigo (with me) | contigo (with you)

::: end
