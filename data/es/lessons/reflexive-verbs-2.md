# Reflexive Verbs II
The placement of reflexive pronouns follows the same rules as direct and indirect object pronouns.

## Placement Rules

- **Before a conjugated verb**: Me levanto temprano.
- **Attached to an infinitive**: Quiero levantarme temprano.
- **Attached to a present participle**: Estoy levantándome temprano.

## Emotional Reflexives

Many verbs in Spanish are reflexive even if they don't seem like an action performed on oneself in English.

- Divertirse (To have fun)
- Aburrirse (To get bored)
- Enojarse (To get angry)
