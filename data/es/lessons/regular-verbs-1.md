# Regular Verbs I (-ar)
In Spanish, verbs are categorized by their endings: -ar, -er, or -ir. This lesson focuses on the most common group: -ar verbs.

## Conjugation Pattern

To conjugate a regular -ar verb in the present tense, remove the -ar to find the stem, then add the following endings:

| Subject | Ending | Example (Hablar) |
|---|---|---|
| Yo | **-o** | hablo |
| Tú | **-as** | hablas |
| Él / Ella / Ud. | **-a** | habla |
| Nosotros/as | **-amos** | hablamos |
| Vosotros/as | **-áis** | habláis |
| Ellos / Ellas / Uds. | **-an** | hablan |
