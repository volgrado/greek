# Relative Pronouns IV
The relative adjective **cuyo** is used to show possession (whose).

## Using Cuyo

Unlike most pronouns, 'cuyo' must agree in gender and number with the **noun that follows it** (the thing owned), not the owner.

- El hombre **cuyo** hijo es médico... (The man whose son is a doctor...)
- La mujer **cuyas** hijas son profesoras... (The woman whose daughters are teachers...)
