# Ser vs Estar III (Uses of Estar)
The verb **estar** is used to describe temporary states, moods, and locations.

### The P.L.A.C.E. Acronym

Use **ESTAR** for:

- **P**osition
- **L**ocation
- **A**ction (Progressive)
- **C**ondition (Physical/Mental)
- **E**motion

## Examples

- Estoy en casa. (I am at home (Location).)
- Él está cansado. (He is tired (Condition).)
- Estamos felices. (We are happy (Emotion).)
