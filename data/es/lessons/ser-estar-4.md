# Ser vs Estar IV (Comparison)
Sometimes, choosing between **ser** and **estar** can change the meaning of an adjective.

## Contrast Table

| Adjective | With Ser | With Estar |
|---|---|---|
| Aburrido | To be boring | To be bored |
| Listo | To be smart | To be ready |
| Rico | To be wealthy | To be delicious |

::: info-box

### Summary Tip

Remember: **Ser** is for WHAT something is, and **Estar** is for HOW something is at the moment.

::: end
