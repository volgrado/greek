# Stem-Changers: e -> i
A smaller group of -ir verbs change the 'e' in the stem to a simple 'i'.

## Example: Pedir (to ask for/order)

| Subject | Conjugation |
|---|---|
| Yo | p**i**do |
| Él / Ella | p**i**de |
| Nosotros | pedimos |
| Ellos | p**i**den |

- Repetir → Repito ((I repeat))
- Servir → Sirvo ((I serve))
