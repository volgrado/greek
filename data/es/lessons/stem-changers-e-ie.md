# Stem-Changers: e -> ie
Stem-changing verbs change their spelling in the root of the word for all forms except nosotros and vosotros.

## Example: Entender (to understand)

| Subject | Conjugation |
|---|---|
| Yo | ent**ie**ndo |
| Tú | ent**ie**ndes |
| Nosotros | entendemos |
| Ellos | ent**ie**nden |

::: info-box

### Boot Verbs

These are often called 'boot verbs' because if you circle the changing forms on a standard grid, it looks like a boot!

::: end
