# Subjunctive: The Six Irregulars
While most subjunctive forms come from the 'yo' form, there are six verbs that are completely irregular. Use the mnemonic **DISISH** to remember them.

## DISISH Verbs

- **D**ar (dé, des, dé...)
- **I**r (vaya, vayas, vaya...)
- **S**er (sea, seas, sea...)
- **I**r (Wait, the second I is for **Ir** again or just a mnemonic? Usually it's Dar, Ir, Ser, Haber, Estar, Saber)
- Let's list them properly below.

## The Big Six

| Verb | Subj. Form (yo) |
|---|---|
| Dar | dé |
| Ir | vaya |
| Ser | sea |
| Haber | haya |
| Estar | esté |
| Saber | sepa |
