# Subjunctive Usage: Doubt
The subjunctive is used to express doubt, denial, or disbelief about a statement.

## Triggers of Doubt

- **Dudar que** (To doubt that)
- **No creer que** (To not believe that)
- **No pensar que** (To not think that)
- **Es imposible que** (It is impossible that)

## Difference

Note the difference between belief and doubt:

- **Creo que** (Fact/Belief) → Indicative
- **No creo que** (Doubt) → Subjunctive
