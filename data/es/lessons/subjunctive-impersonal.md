# Subjunctive Usage: Impersonal Expressions
Impersonal expressions (It is + adjective + that) often trigger the subjunctive, provided they don't express certainty.

## Triggers

- **Es necesario que** (It is necessary that)
- **Es importante que** (It is important that)
- **Es posible que** (It is possible that)
- **Es una lástima que** (It is a pity that)

## The Exception: Certainty

Expressions that imply certainty or truth use the **indicative**.

- **Es verdad que** (It is true that) + indicative
- **Es cierto que** (It is certain that) + indicative
