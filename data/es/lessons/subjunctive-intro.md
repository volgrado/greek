# Subjunctive Intro
The subjunctive is NOT a tense; it's a **mood**. It reflects the speaker's attitude toward an action.

## Mood vs Tense

A tense tells us **when** something happens (past, present, future). A mood tells us **how** the speaker feels about it.

- **Indicative**: Used for facts and reality (I go, he ate).
- **Subjunctive**: Used for hope, doubt, emotion, and uncertainty (I hope he goes).

::: info-box

### The Trigger Rule

For the subjunctive to be used in a dependent clause, you usually need:

A main clause with a 'trigger' verb.The connector 'que'.A change of subject.
::: end
