# Subjunctive: Stem-Changers
Stem-changing verbs in the subjunctive follow the same pattern as in the present indicative, with a small exception for -ir verbs.

## -ar and -er Verbs

These verbs change their stem in all forms EXCEPT nosotros (just like the present tense).

piense, pienses, piense, **pensemos**, piensen

## -ir Verbs

These verbs change their stem in all forms, but in the nosotros form, the change is slightly different.

- e → ie (indicative) becomes **e → i** in nosotros.
- o → ue (indicative) becomes **o → u** in nosotros.

::: example-box
Sintamos (from sentir) | Durmamos (from dormir)

::: end
