# Subjunctive Usage: Wishes
One of the primary uses of the subjunctive is to express wishes, wants, or desires for someone else.

## Trigger Verbs

These verbs in the main clause trigger the subjunctive in the dependent clause:

- **Querer que** (To want that)
- **Desear que** (To desire that)
- **Esperar que** (To hope that)

## The Pattern

::: example-box
Quiero **que** tú **vengas**. (I want you to come.)

::: end

Note: If there is no change of subject, use the infinitive (Quiero venir).
