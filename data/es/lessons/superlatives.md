# Superlatives
Superlatives are used to describe something that has the highest or lowest degree of a quality (the most, the least).

## The Formula

Formula: **definite article + [noun] + más/menos + [adjective] + de**

- Juan es el más alto de la clase. (Juan is the tallest in the class.)
- Es la película menos interesante de todas. (It is the least interesting movie of all.)

### Absolute Superlatives

To say something is 'extremely' or 'very' [adjective], you can add the suffix **-ísimo/a/os/as** to the adjective.

rápido → rapidísimo | inteligente → inteligentísimo
