# Verbs like Gustar
Many other verbs in Spanish follow the same 'backwards' structure as gustar.

## Common Verbs

| Verb | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Fascinar | To fascinate |
| Encantar | To love/delight |
| Importar | To matter/be important |
| Molestar | To bother/annoy |
| Quedar | To remain/to be left |

## Examples

- Me encanta el chocolate. (I love chocolate.)
- ¿Te molesta el ruido? (Does the noise bother you?)
