Travel Dialogues

When traveling in Greece, knowing how to ask for directions and handle common travel situations is essential.

Πού είναι η τουαλέτα; Where is the bathroom?Πού είναι η στάση; Where is the bus stop?Πού είναι η έξοδος; Where is the exit? ::: end

Asking How to Get Somewhere

Πώς πάω στην πλατεία; How do I get to the square?Πώς πάω στην Ακρόπολη; How do I get to the Acropolis? ::: end

Travel Tip

In Greece, a nod of the head upward often means "No" (Όχι), while a nod downward means "Yes" (Ναι). This can be confusing for visitors at first!
