Direct Object Pronouns II

When a sentence contains two verbs (like 'I want to see it'), you have a choice of where to place the pronoun.

Placement with Infinitives

Option 1: Before the conjugated verb.

Lo quiero ver.

Option 2: Attached to the end of the infinitive.

Quiero verlo.

Both are correct!

Spanish speakers use both forms, although attaching it to the end is often considered more natural in spoken Spanish for some learners.
