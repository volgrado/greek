Hace meaning 'Ago'

The verb 'hacer' is used in several time-related expressions, most notably to mean 'ago'.

Time Passed

Pattern: Hace + Time + que + Present Verb

To express how long you have BEEN doing something:

Hace dos años que vivo aquí. (I have lived here for two years.)

Actions in the Past ('Ago')

Pattern: Preterite Verb + hace + Time

Fui a España hace tres meses. (I went to Spain three months ago.)
