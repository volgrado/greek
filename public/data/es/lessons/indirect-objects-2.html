Indirect Object Pronouns II

Because le and les can refer to many different people, Spanish often uses extra phrases for clarity.

Clarity Phrases

Formula: [Pronoun] ... a + [noun/pronoun]

  • Yo le doy el libro a ella. (I give the book to her.)
  • Él les habla a los estudiantes. (He speaks to the students.)

Redundancy

In Spanish, the indirect object pronoun is often used even when the noun is also present in the sentence. This is perfectly normal and required in many contexts.
