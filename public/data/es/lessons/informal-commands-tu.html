Informal Commands: Tú

Affirmative informal commands are used with friends, family, and people you know well.

The Regular Rule

For regular verbs, use the third-person singular form of the present tense (the él/ella form).

  • Hablar → Habla (Speak!)
  • Comer → Come (Eat!)
  • Escribir → Escribe (Write!)

Irregular Tú Commands

There are 8 common irregular commands in the 'tú' form:

Decir → di | Hacer → haz | Ir → ve

Poner → pon | Salir → sal | Ser → sé

Tener → ten | Venir → ven
