Intro to the Preterite

The preterite tense is used to describe actions that were completed in the past at a specific point in time.

When to Use

  • Actions that occurred once.
  • Actions that were part of a chain of events.
  • Actions that occurred within a specific time frame.

Keywords

If you see these words, you usually need the preterite:

ayer (yesterday), anoche (last night), la semana pasada (last week).
