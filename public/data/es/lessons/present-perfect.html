The Present Perfect

The present perfect is used to talk about actions that have happened in the past and continue to be relevant in the present (I have eaten).

The Formula

Formula: Present tense of HABER + Past Participle

Person Haber
yo he
tú has
él/ella ha
nosotros hemos
ellos/ellas han

Forming Regular Past Participles

  • -ar verbs → -ado (hablar → hablado)
  • -er/-ir verbs → -ido (comer → comido / vivir → vivido)

::: example-box He hablado con mi padre. (I have spoken with my father.)
