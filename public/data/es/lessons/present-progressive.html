Present Progressive

The present progressive is used to describe an action that is happening right now.

The Formula

Formula: Estar + Present Participle (-ando / -iendo)

  • For -ar verbs: replace -ar with -ando (hablar → hablando)
  • For -er/-ir verbs: replace -er/-ir with -iendo (comer → comiendo / vivir → viviendo)

Examples

  • Estoy hablando por teléfono. (I am speaking on the phone.)
  • ¿Qué estás comiendo? (What are you eating?)
