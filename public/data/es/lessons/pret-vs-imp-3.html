Preterite vs Imperfect III

Some verbs change their English translation depending on whether you use the preterite or the imperfect.

Meaning Changes

Verb Imperfect Preterite
Saber Knew (info) Found out
Conocer Knew (person) Met (for first time)
Querer Wanted Tried to
Poder Was able to Succeeded/Managed to

Tip

The preterite often focuses on the start or finish of an action, while the imperfect focuses on the state.
