Reflexive Verbs I

Reflexive verbs indicate that the subject performs an action on themselves.

The Pronouns

Reflexive verbs are always accompanied by these pronouns:

yo me
tú te
él/ella se
nosotros nos
ellos/ellas se

Example: Lavarse (To wash oneself)

  • Me lavo las manos. (I wash my hands.)
  • Se lava la cara. (He/She washes their face.)

Identifying Reflexives

In their infinitive form, reflexive verbs always end in -se (e.g., lavarse, ducharse, levantarse).
