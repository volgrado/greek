Regular Verbs III (-ir)

Regular -ir verbs are almost identical to -er verbs in their endings, with exceptions in the 'nosotros' and 'vosotros' forms.

Conjugation Pattern

To conjugate a regular -ir verb in the present tense, remove the -ir to find the stem, then add the following endings:

Subject Ending Example (Vivir)
Yo -o vivo
Tú -es vives
Él / Ella / Ud. -e vive
Nosotros/as -imos vivimos
Vosotros/as -ís vivís
Ellos / Ellas / Uds. -en viven
