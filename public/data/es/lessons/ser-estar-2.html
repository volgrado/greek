Ser vs Estar II (Uses of Ser)

The verb ser is used to describe permanent or essential qualities.

The D.O.C.T.O.R. Acronym

Use SER for:

  • Description (Physical/Personality)
  • Ocupation
  • Characteristic
  • Time
  • Origin
  • Relationship

Examples

  • Yo soy profesor. (I am a teacher (Occupation).)
  • Ella es de Madrid. (She is from Madrid (Origin).)
  • Hoy es lunes. (Today is Monday (Time).)
