Subject Pronouns

Subject pronouns are the words that tell us who is performing the action of the verb.

Person Singular Plural
1st Person yo (I) nosotros/as (we)
2nd Person (Informal) tú (you) vosotros/as (you all)
3rd Person / Formal él / ella / usted (he/she/you formal) ellos / ellas / ustedes (they/you all formal)

Did you know?

In many parts of the Spanish-speaking world, ustedes is used for both formal and informal 'you all,' and vosotros is primarily used in Spain.
