Subjunctive Usage: Emotion

When the main clause expresses a feeling or emotion about an action, the subjunctive is used in the second clause.

Emotional Triggers

  • Sentir que (To be sorry that)
  • Alegrarse de que (To be glad that)
  • Temer que (To fear that)
  • Tener miedo de que (To be afraid that)

Example

Me alegro de que estés aquí.

I am glad that you are here.

Note

Just like with wishes, if there is no change of subject, use the infinitive: Me alegro de estar aquí.
