Subjunctive: Present

To form the present subjunctive, use the same 'opposite ending' logic as formal commands.

The Formula

  1. Go to the yo form of the present indicative.

  2. Drop the -o.

  3. Add the 'opposite' endings.

Person -ar verbs -er/-ir verbs
yo -e -a
tú -es -as
él/ella -e -a
nosotros -emos -amos
ellos -en -an
