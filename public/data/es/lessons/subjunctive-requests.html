Subjunctive Usage: Requests

Verbs that express a request, command, advice, or suggestion for another person trigger the subjunctive.

Request/Influence Triggers

  • Pedir que (To ask that)
  • Decir que (To tell [someone to do something])
  • Recomendar que (To recommend that)
  • Sugerir que (To suggest that)

Example

Él me pide que estudie más.

He asks me to study more.
