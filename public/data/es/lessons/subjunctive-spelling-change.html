Subjunctive: Spelling Changes

Just like with preterite and formal commands, certain verbs require a spelling change in the subjunctive to maintain their sound.

The -car, -gar, -zar rule

  • -car → qu (sacar → saque)
  • -gar → gu (pagar → pague)
  • -zar → c (almorzar → almuerce)

Note

Unlike the preterite (where the change only happens in the 'yo' form), in the subjunctive, the change happened in all forms.
